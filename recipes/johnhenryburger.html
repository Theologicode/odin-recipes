John Henry Burger

Description

Hamburgers are an American staple food. Everybody has their own take on the hamburger, including fast food chains like McDonald's and Burger King, Whattaburger, and Jack-In-The-Box as well as restaurants like T.G.I. Friday's and Au Chevalle. American homes are blessed with many varieties of head-of-household recipes, including this one.

Ingredients

  • One Brioche Hamburger Bun
  • Butter
  • 12oz jalapeno pepper juice
  • one sliced jalapeno
  • 2 slices Monterrey Jack cheese
  • two all beef paddies
  • jalapeno mayonaise

Steps

  1. Marinate both all beef paddies in 12oz. Jalapeno juice over night.
  2. Cook paddies to taste with no other seasoning
  3. heat brioche bun in oven or toaster
  4. slather bun with butter
  5. place paddies on bun, dress with jalapeno mayonaise, and as many jalapeno peppers as one can take.
